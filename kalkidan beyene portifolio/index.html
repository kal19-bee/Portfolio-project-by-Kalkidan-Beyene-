Treatment

Treatment of neurological disorders often involves a combination of medication, physical therapy, and lifestyle
changes to manage symptoms and improve quality of life.
In more complex cases, surgical interventions or advanced therapies like deep brain stimulation may be used.
Early diagnosis and personalized care plans are essential for effective long-term management.

Learn More

Consultancy

A consultant neurologist typically leads clinical teams, mentors junior doctors, and may work in both hospital and private practice settings
Their work blends deep medical expertise with strategic decision-making to guide patient care and improve neurological outcomes.

Learn More

Training

Neurology training begins with medical school,
where students learn the foundations of human biology and clinical care. After graduation, they enter a neurology residency to gain hands-on experience diagnosing and treating brain and nerve disorders.
Some pursue advanced fellowships to specialize further and become leaders in research, surgery, or patient care.
